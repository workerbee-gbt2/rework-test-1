# Company Profile: Team, Funding, and Competition

Details on the team, funding history, investors, and competitive landscape.

## Funding

Browser-use has raised a total of $17.5 million in funding [1](#ref-1) [2](#ref-2). 

### Funding Rounds

*   **Seed Round:** Raised $17 million in March 2025 [1](#ref-1) [2](#ref-2) [3](#ref-3). The round was led by Felicis Ventures (specifically Astasia Myers) [1](#ref-1) [2](#ref-2).
*   **Previous Funding:** Approximately $500,000 was previously raised, likely corresponding to the company's participation in Y Combinator [2](#ref-2) [4](#ref-4).

### Investors

Investors in Browser-use include:
*   Felicis Ventures (Lead) [1](#ref-1)
*   Paul Graham [2](#ref-2)
*   A Capital [2](#ref-2)
*   Nexus Venture Partners [2](#ref-2)
*   Liquid2 Ventures [3](#ref-3)
*   SV Angel [3](#ref-3)
*   Pioneer Fund [3](#ref-3)
*   Y Combinator [4](#ref-4)
*   468 Capital [3](#ref-3)
*   Lombardstreet Ventures [3](#ref-3)
